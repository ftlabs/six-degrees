Six degrees of Angela Merkel

The idea of being a certain number of steps away from well connected individuals is pretty common. Mathematician Paul Erdős collaborated with so many peers that the Erdős number came to be known as the 'collaborative distance' between Erdős and any other academic. It's more well known cousin, the Bacon number, came 25 years later, to describe the collaborative distance between Kevin Bacon and other actors.
